About Us

RustBooks is a passion-driven initiative founded by Anirudha Gaikwad and shaped by the minds of creators, educators, and technologists who believe in learning that’s both beautiful and accessible. We’re dedicated to empowering learners with Rust programming skills through high-quality books, interactive courses, and real-world projects. Our mission is to make Rust education inclusive—with multilingual resources in Marathi, Hindi, and English—designed to resonate with institutions, beginners, and professionals alike.
